# Outcome-led Project Copy Design

## Goal

Rewrite the RQAI hub so each project page sells the outcome, distinctive capabilities and sense of possibility before explaining implementation details. Reduce visible prose by roughly 40–50% without removing important, verified features.

## Voice

Use a balanced voice tailored to the product:

- Clinical and research tools begin with a recognisable frustration, then establish capability, rigour and user control.
- Creative tools begin with an inviting possibility, then reveal expressive and surprising things the user can make.
- Use British English, plain language and specific verbs.
- Avoid hype, guarantees, vague superlatives and claims that cannot be verified in the product source.
- AI is presented as optional assistance. Never imply that a product eliminates hallucinations or replaces professional judgement.

## Project Page Structure

Each page will use this content hierarchy:

1. **Recognition line** — one audience-specific frustration or ambition.
2. **Product promise** — 35–60 visible words describing the outcome.
3. **Feature moments** — three to five titled capabilities. Each has a short, benefit-led heading and one concise proof sentence.
4. **Proof strip** — scannable numbers, formats, platforms or validated capabilities where the source supports them.
5. **Control note** — one compact statement covering privacy, ownership, local operation or optional AI.
6. **Price and action** — one clear price and CTA treatment.

The existing longer metadata description may remain more detailed for search and structured data, but it must not be used as the visible opening paragraph.

## ResearchAssistant Emphasis

ResearchAssistant will lead with evidence control and optional AI, then give substantial, equal visibility to its real research workbench:

- systematic review from search strategy through screening, risk of bias, meta-analysis and GRADE;
- statistical analysis, including tests validated against equivalent runs in R;
- health-economic analysis, including QALY, ICER/NMB, CEAC, sensitivity analysis and CHEERS-style reporting;
- evidence-grounded drafting and the ability to work without generative AI;
- Reviewer 2 critique and submission preparation.

Suggested opening direction:

> **Research without surrendering control to AI.**
>
> Tired of confident claims your evidence cannot support? Use AI to simplify the small steps, trace generated statements back to your own library, or switch it off entirely. ResearchAssistant still carries the work from systematic review and statistical or economic analysis through to a submission-ready manuscript.

This language may be refined during implementation, but its factual meaning and hierarchy are fixed.

## Product Story Angles

- **ClinicalPROMs:** understand recovery and collect validated outcomes without giving patient data to a vendor platform.
- **Chapbook:** own a beautiful blog and publish as naturally as messaging; change themes, add rich and interactive material, create flipbook-style animation, and use verified ideation tools such as Goblin mode if present in the source.
- **OrthoPortfolio:** turn an intimidating evidence pile into a structured, redacted and submission-ready Portfolio Pathway application.
- **OrthoConsultantPrep:** prepare for the questions and published signals of a specific Trust, not a generic interview.
- **AudioQuill:** capture ideas at speaking speed and turn them into structured documents without losing the original words.
- **Scribble:** let notes grow into visual, interactive and publishable work while retaining control of the files and sync.

## Verification Rules

- Inspect the current source, README, shipped-feature records or live application for every promoted capability.
- Do not rely on an old marketing page when current product source contradicts it.
- Preserve every route, project URL, price, checkout hand-off and structured-data field unless a verified correction is required.
- Features that cannot be verified are omitted or described more cautiously.
- Add copy-contract tests for the new visible structure and ResearchAssistant’s systematic-review, statistics and economics emphasis.

## Scope

Rewrite the visible Home introduction, seven project-page openings and feature presentations, and About-page prose where it repeats the old implementation-led voice. Preserve the current visual identity, animation system, navigation and external-link behaviour except for layout changes necessary to present titled feature moments and proof strips.
